Minigame: Astraburst!


What is Astraburst?

Astraburst is a game of guessing the safe tiles in 'The Asteroid Belt' (a minefield) . This is a single-player game. Read the rules and proceed!

How to Play:

  1. Select a size for the playing field. This can be any among 6x6 and 7x7.
  2. Once selected, the playing field should generate. Hovering the mouse over a tile on the playing field should cause changes.
  3. Click a tile to select it. If the tile is asteroid-free, then your score shall be incremented by 1. Once clicked, the same tile can't be selected again.
  4. If there is an asteroid on a selected tile, it is equivalent to a crash! This now freezes your score. There are 11 asteroids. You can hit the reset button to play again.
  5. Have fun!
