Mail IITB

Enhancing Academic Productivity for the IITB Community

Student Initiative

Smart Webmail, Unified Schedule.

Mail IITB is a browser extension developed to help students manage high volumes of academic correspondence by integrating AI summarization with Google Calendar.

Summarize

Leverage Gemini-Flash-2.5 to turn long webmail threads into concise, actionable bullet points instantly.

Sync

Identify deadlines and meeting invites within emails and add them to your Google Calendar with a single click.

Draft

Generate context-aware email responses to save time on routine academic communication.

Google Calendar Integration

Mail IITB requests Google Calendar API access to create and manage events. We access your calendar data only when you explicitly click to schedule a task. Your data is processed in real-time and is never stored on our servers or used for AI model training.
