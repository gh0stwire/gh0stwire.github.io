Privacy Policy

For Google Calendar Integration

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Webmail Browser Extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service with Google Calendar.

By using our service, you grant us permission to access and manage your Google Calendar data as described in this policy.

2. Information We Access

Through Google Calendar API, we request access to:

  • View your calendars and events
  • Create, edit, or delete events
  • Read event details including titles, descriptions, times, and locations
  • Manage calendar settings (only if required for specific functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use Google Calendar data solely to:

  • Schedule events interpreted directly off read mails on button-click
  • Sync events and their descriptions as and when updated
  • Perform actions you explicitly request (creating/modifying events)

We Do NOT:

  • Share your calendar data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Store your calendar data permanently on our servers

4. Data Storage & Security

Your calendar data is handled with utmost care:

  • Data is processed in real-time via secure Google APIs
  • We may cache limited data temporarily for performance (automatically deleted after 1 hour)
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

5. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Your Control & Permissions

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke our access anytime via Google Security Settings
  • Control what calendar data is shared with us
  • Request deletion of any stored data by contacting us
